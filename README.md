# Team TechCore

Welcome to the official GitHub organization of **Team TechCore**.

We are a team of Computer Science and Engineering students focused on learning, building, and improving real-world software solutions. Our work includes academic projects, web applications, software tools, and collaborative development experiments.

## About Us

**Team TechCore** is built around teamwork, practical learning, and problem-solving. We believe that strong software is created through clean code, clear communication, and continuous improvement.

Our team members work together on different projects based on their skills, interests, and responsibilities. As students, we are constantly exploring new technologies, improving our development practices, and turning ideas into working applications.

## Institution

We are Computer Science and Engineering students of **United International University**.
