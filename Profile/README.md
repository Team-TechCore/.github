# Team TechCore

Welcome to the official GitHub of **Team TechCore**.

We are a team of Computer Science and Engineering students focused on learning, building, researching, and improving real-world software solutions. Our work includes academic projects, research-based development, AI-powered systems, web applications, software tools, IoT projects, robotics, and collaborative development experiments.

## About Us

**Team TechCore** is built around teamwork, practical learning, research, and problem-solving. We believe that strong software is created through clean code, clear communication, continuous improvement, and a research-driven mindset.

Our team members work together on different projects based on their skills, interests, and responsibilities. As students, we are constantly exploring new technologies, improving our development practices, and turning ideas into working applications.

Alongside software development, we are also focused on research-oriented projects, especially in areas such as Large Language Models (LLMs), Small Language Models (SLMs), Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), Artificial Intelligence, IoT, and Robotics.

## Institution

We are Computer Science and Engineering students of **United International University**.
